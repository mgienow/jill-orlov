▣ CONTAINED: Miniature Objects of Art

Miniature kitchen in a vintage Frank’s beverage wood crate.

Can be hung on wall or sit on shelf for display.

16-1/2″ wide x 12-3/4″ tall x 4-1/2″ deep


The hanging 100% copper pots are made from plumbing parts, sure to delight miniature lovers. All appliances made of steel (no, not stainless steel). Each item is carefully handcrafted, attentive to every detail. Perfect for the home chef, foodie or even adorning the walls of a restaurant.


Soon to be on display in the American Visionary Art Museum in Baltimore, Maryland. Custom commissions are available! If you have a robin’s egg blue retro refrigerator, a kitchen island, a built-in banquette...a completely custom kitchen room can be created just for you.
